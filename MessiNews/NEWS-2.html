ARGENTINA CLAIM FINALISSIMA 2022 CROWN

The Albiceleste ran out 3-0 winners over the Azzurri at Wembley. Leo captained Argentina and set them on their way to victory by providing the assist for the opening goal in the 28th minute. This triumph sees Leo and Argentina add another title to their haul on the back of last year's Copa América success.
