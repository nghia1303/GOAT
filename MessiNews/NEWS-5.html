LEO SET FOR LAST LEAGUE OUTING OF SEASON

Leo Messi and his PSG teammates face Metz at the Parc des Princes in the final matchday of the Ligue 1 campaign. The Parisians will bring the curtain down on the season, in which they were crowned champions, on home soil. Messi had two goals ruled out during that contest, and has now picked up a knock that will keep him out of PSG's next outing at the Stade Raymond Kopa - where they could clinch the trophy with five games to spare.
