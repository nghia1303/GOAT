LEO HITS FIVE IN WIN OVER ESTONIA

Argentina ran out 5-0 winners over Estonia at El Sadar, in the Spanish city of Pamplona, in a friendly match in preparation for the 2022 Qatar World Cup. Leo completed the full 90 minutes and scored all five of the Albiceleste’s goals. It is the second time in his career that the Argentina captain has notched five goals in a single game after having previously achieved the feat for FC Barcelona in the Champions League meeting with Bayer Leverkusen in March 2012.
